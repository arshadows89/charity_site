The best way to get invloved is start with your own selves. We who started this website first started with our habbits as to how we buy, consume and how much we keep for leftover. THe aim is to keep your food wasting to a minimum, to start key things to remember:

  • Plan ahead
  • Buy what you need
  • Store correctly
  • Cook the right amount
  • Eat it all or store leftover
  • Recycle what you can't eat
